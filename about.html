Who Are We?

Dream destinations is your go-to resource for planning a trip to Sri Lanka which is a touring agency formed by a group of undergraduate students and professionals.
Our website features a wide range of information on the top attractions, adventure activities, upcoming events, a blog of popular tourist destinations, and traditional products such as masks, spices, arts, foods, industrial goods and etc. Whether you're looking to relax on beautiful beaches, explore ancient temples, or discover the local cuisine, Dream Destinations has you covered. We can't wait to help you plan an unforgettable trip to Sri Lanka.

About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, known as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a stunning tropical paradise offering a rich blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and adventure. From its pristine beaches and lush tea plantations to its ancient temples and vibrant wildlife, Sri Lanka provides visitors with an unforgettable experience. Whether you're exploring the historical cities of Kandy and Anuradhapura, hiking through the misty highlands, or encountering elephants in national parks, Sri Lanka is a destination that captivates the heart and soul. Its warm hospitality, delicious cuisine, and colorful festivals make it a must-visit for travelers seeking both relaxation and exploration.
